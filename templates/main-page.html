About us

We are Alanna Lu and Cheryl Chen, two Grade 12 students at Earl of March S.S. When deciding on a project theme, Alanna mentioned food delivery as a joke, but it managed to spark a brilliant thought in our minds: Meal Tinder, a program that lets you "match" with your meals. We are hardworking #womeninSTEM, who have been developing this project for the past 4.5 months. We hope you appreciate this website as much as we do. Without further ado, welcome to Meal Tinder.
